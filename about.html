About

I'm a student in the AI-Native program with a journey that started far from Silicon Valley. Originally from Russia, I've called NYC home since 2005.

My tech story began unexpectedly in 2017 when I landed a nutritionist role at Plated, a food tech company. Working in that environment opened my eyes to how technology could revolutionize industries I was passionate about.

After a few years at Plated, I decided to pivot and learn how to code. I joined Pursuit's iOS developer program (the same amazing school where I'm now diving into AI), which led me to work as an iOS developer at Twitter.

Now I'm back at Pursuit exploring how AI is transforming development and learning to build smarter, more efficient solutions.

When I'm not experimenting with AI tools or coding, you'll find me in the kitchen, planning my next travel adventure, or trying to convince my 16-year-old son that AI could revolutionize his homework process (still working on that one).

Oh, and yes—that's my cat photobombing the my GitHub link. He insisted on being part of my AI-powered developer journey.

Currently learning: How to leverage AI for web development, prompt engineering, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and discovering new ways AI can enhance every step of the development process.
